### [1] [Primordial Black Hole interpretation of the sub-solar merger event S251112cm](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25795)
*Md Riajul Haque,Fabio Iocco,Luca Visinelli*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA候选事件S251112cm显示存在亚太阳质量致密天体，这可能是原初黑洞合并的证据。研究分析了该事件作为两个0.1-1太阳质量原初黑洞合并的概率，发现在某些约束条件下概率可达1，表明原初黑洞解释在当前约束下是可行的。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: S251112cm事件中观测到亚太阳质量致密天体，这无法通过标准恒星演化形成，因此需要探索非标准形成机制。原初黑洞作为早期宇宙密度涨落坍缩形成的候选者，是解释这类事件的潜在机制。

Method: 结合原初黑洞合并率的解析估计、当前对其丰度的观测约束以及LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA搜索的灵敏度，计算观测到S251112cm这类事件的概率。考虑了宽松和保守两种约束场景。

Result: 在宽松约束场景下，原初黑洞质量在0.5-1太阳质量范围内时，观测到该事件的概率可达1；在更保守场景和较低质量下，概率仍保持约0.5的量级。这表明原初黑洞解释在当前约束下是可行的。

Conclusion: S251112cm事件的原初黑洞解释在当前约束下是合理的，但由于存在天体物理不确定性（如原初黑洞丰度约束），不能作为确定性结论。同时，亚太阳质量引力波事件具有作为探测原初黑洞及其对暗物质贡献的潜力。

Abstract: The LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA (LVK) candidate event S251112cm suggests the presence of at least one compact object with sub-solar masses. Since such objects cannot be produced through standard stellar evolution, this observation provides a potential indication of non-standard formation channels. Primordial black holes (PBHs), formed from the collapse of primordial density fluctuations in the early Universe, are a well-motivated candidate. We investigate the interpretation of S251112cm as the merger of two PBHs with masses in the range 0.1-1$\,M_\odot$. Combining analytic estimates of the PBH merger rate with current observational constraints on their abundance and the sensitivity of LVK searches, we compute the probability of observing such an event. Within a relaxed constraint scenario, the probability reaches unity in the range $M_{\rm PBH} \sim 0.5$-$1\,M_\odot$, while it remains sizable, $\sim \mathcal{O}(0.5)$, in more conservative scenarios and at lower masses. Our results show that a PBH interpretation of S251112cm is viable within current bounds. Owing to the dependence of our results on astrophysical uncertainties, such as those affecting the constraints on the abundance of PBHs, they cannot be regarded as conclusive with respect to the nature of the detected event. At the same time, our analysis highlights the potential of sub-solar gravitational wave events as a probe of PBHs and their contribution to dark matter.

### [2] [A Bell Experiment in an Entangled Universe](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25879)

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种早期宇宙的量子特征，通过引力子与暴胀场的相互作用产生纠缠态，在宇宙学观测中留下可测量的量子印记。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索早期宇宙的量子性质，特别是暴胀时期的量子特征如何在现代宇宙学观测中留下可检测的印记。

Method: 利用暴胀标量场的经典相干态产生引力子，通过引力子偏振态的纠缠，结合视界交叉时的相互作用和"路径信息"收集，在标量关联四点函数中实现贝尔实验测量。

Result: 发现引力子与暴胀场的相互作用会在标量关联函数中产生非平凡效应，这种效应依赖于引力子的偏振测量，为早期宇宙量子特征提供了可观测的指纹。

Conclusion: 该研究提出了一种通过高阶星系相关函数（特别是晕偏和本征排列）来测量早期宇宙量子特征的新方法，为探索暴胀时期的量子性质开辟了新途径。

Abstract: We propose a possible quantum signature of the early Universe that could lead to observational imprints of the quantum nature of the inflationary period. Graviton production from the presence of a classical, coherent state of the inflaton scalar field results in entangled states in the gravitons' polarizations. At horizon crossing, interactions between the gravitons and (lower scale) inflatons, together with the gathering of ``which-path information'' from the cosmological horizon, perform the required Bell experiments leading to a definitive measure, which can be imprinted in the scalar correlation four-point function. This is because of a non-trivial effect due to the derivatives on two scalar fluctuations, and it provides a fingerprint that depends on the polarization of the graviton that Alice and/or Bob measured in their patch. We hint how this signature could be measured in the high-order correlation function of galaxies, in particular on the halo bias and the intrinsic alignment.

### [3] [Cosmological constraints on the big bang quantum cosmology model](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25999)

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: JCDM宇宙学模型使用Schouten张量的迹J作为动态暗能量，结合冷暗物质，试图解决哈勃张力。研究利用CMB、DESI BAO、CC和SNIa数据约束JCDM模型，发现平坦宇宙下H0=66.95±0.51 km/s/Mpc，Ωm=0.3419±0.0065；非平坦宇宙下Ωk=0.0154±0.0027，H0=69.13±0.56 km/s/Mpc，显示几何简并性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: JCDM宇宙学模型由大爆炸量子宇宙学提出，将Schouten张量的迹J作为动态暗能量形式，旨在解决当前宇宙学中的哈勃张力问题。该模型基于第一性原理构建，除了ΛCDM参数外没有额外自由参数。

Method: 使用早期和晚期宇宙学数据对JCDM模型进行约束分析，包括宇宙微波背景辐射（CMB）、暗能量光谱仪（DESI）发布的重子声学振荡（BAO）、宇宙计时器（CC）和Ia型超新星（SNIa）。分别考虑平坦宇宙和非平坦宇宙两种情况。

Result: 平坦宇宙下：H0=66.95±0.51 km/s/Mpc，Ωm=0.3419±0.0065，与早期宇宙观测一致但Ωm高于ΛCDM模型。非平坦宇宙下：Ωk=0.0154±0.0027（轻微弯曲几何），H0=69.13±0.56 km/s/Mpc，Ωm=0.3477±0.0074，显示空间曲率与H0之间的几何简并性。JCDM模型与晚期宇宙学数据吻合良好，但难以同时与早期宇宙观测完全自洽。

Conclusion: JCDM宇宙学模型虽然基于第一性原理构建且与晚期宇宙观测数据吻合良好，但在同时匹配早期宇宙观测方面存在困难。非平坦宇宙情形下H0的增加表明空间曲率与哈勃常数之间存在几何简并性。该模型为解决哈勃张力提供了一种理论途径，但仍需进一步完善以实现早期和晚期宇宙观测的完全自洽。

Together with cold dark matter, these components form the so-called $J$CDM cosmology model, proposed by M.H.P.M. van Putten (J. High Energy Astrophys., 45, 2025, 194), which offers a potential resolution to the Hubble tension. We derive the constraints on the $J$CDM cosmology model, utilizing early- and late-time cosmological data including cosmic microwave background (CMB), baryon acoustic oscillations (BAO) released by the Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument (DESI), cosmic chronometers (CC), and type Ia supernovae (SNIa). For a flat universe, the $J$CDM model yields \( H_0 = 66.95 \pm 0.51 \, \rm{km~s^{-1}~Mpc^{-1}} \) and \( Ω_m = 0.3419 \pm 0.0065 \), results that are consistent with early-universe observations but exhibit a higher \( Ω_m \) compared to the $Λ$CDM model. The increase in \( H_0 \) in the non-flat scenario suggests a geometric degeneracy between spatial curvature and \( H_0 \). We also investigate the internal inconsistencies present in DESI data and evaluate their impacts on cosmological parameter constraints. Our analysis shows that while the $J$CDM model, which is constructed from first principles without free parameters beyond those of $Λ$CDM, agrees excellently with late-time cosmology, it struggles to simultaneously match early-universe observations in a fully self-consistent manner.

### [4] [A Bell experiment during inflation: probing quantum entanglement in tensor fluctuations through correlations of primordial scalar curvature perturbations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25881)
*Pablo Tejerina-Pérez,Leonid Sarieddine,Daniele Bertacca,Raul Jimenez*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种通过观测原初涨落来检验其量子起源的方法，利用标量-张量相互作用将引力子偏振纠缠信息转移到标量场，构建可违反贝尔不等式的八点关联函数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机是寻找原初涨落的量子起源的观测特征。在暴胀期间产生的原初扰动通常被认为是量子起源的，但如何通过观测来验证这一量子性质一直是一个挑战。作者希望通过构建可违反贝尔不等式的可观测量来直接检验原初涨落的量子性。

Method: 方法基于最小单场暴胀模型，考虑偏振态纠缠的引力子对。通过两个标量场和一个引力子的三阶相互作用，将引力子的偏振信息转移到标量场。作者计算了原初标量八点关联函数的张量偏振结构，该函数在傅里叶空间中分解为四个标量两点关联函数的乘积，对应镜像动量配置。这种分解源于两个引力子在暴胀宇宙视界内空间分离，类似于标准贝尔实验的设置。

Result: 结果表明，对于暴胀结束后特定的标量动量配置，该可观测量可以构建出违反贝尔不等式的量，具体形式与著名的Clauser-Horne-Shimony-Holt不等式定义相匹配。这为通过现今可观测的量来探测原初涨落的量子性质提供了一条途径。

Conclusion: 该研究提出了一种通过观测原初标量涨落来检验其量子起源的新方法。通过分析标量八点关联函数中的偏振纠缠特征，可以构建违反贝尔不等式的可观测量，从而为验证原初涨落的量子性质提供了潜在的观测途径。

Abstract: We propose a method that provides an observational signature of the quantum origin of primordial fluctuations generated during inflation. The method gives a prescription for testing a Bell inequality constructed exclusively from the standard scalar and tensor perturbations of minimal single-field inflation. We consider an inflationary spacetime populated by pairs of gravitons entangled in their polarization states. Third-order interactions between two scalars and one graviton transfer polarization information to the scalar sector through the product of spatial derivatives of scalars with the tensor polarization factors. Rather than performing the full multidimensional momentum integrations, we isolate and compute the tensor polarization structure of the primordial scalar eight-point correlation function. This eight-point correlation function factorizes into the product of four scalar two-point functions associated with opposite (mirrored) momentum configurations in Fourier space. This factorization falls from the fact that the two gravitons are spatially well-separated within the cosmological horizon of inflation, replicating the setup of standard Bell experiments. Through these interactions, we track how non-local correlations between both gravitons from polarization entanglement are imprinted on the scalar sector. We show that, for specific configurations of the scalar momenta after the end of inflation (detailed in the text), this observable can be used to construct a Bell-violating quantity in a way that matches the well-known Clauser-Horne-Shimony-Holt inequality definition. In principle, this offers a route to probe the quantum nature of primordial fluctuations through observables accessible today.

### [5] [Population III star formation in an X-ray background: IV. On-the-fly calculation of radiation backgrounds and their impact on the intergalactic medium](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26352)
*Jongwon Park,Massimo Ricotti*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该研究通过宇宙学放大模拟，探讨了仅由Pop III超新星产生的X射线对星际介质电离和加热的影响，发现这种最小化X射线反馈模型能增强分子氢形成和Pop III恒星形成，特别是在低密度区域。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究X射线源（特别是Pop III超新星）对星际介质的电离和加热作用，以及由此产生的分子氢形成和Pop III恒星形成的增强效应，旨在理解早期宇宙中恒星形成的反馈机制。

Method: 采用宇宙学放大模拟方法，在宇宙平均密度区域进行模拟，实时计算全局X射线和莱曼-维尔纳辐射背景，采用最小化X射线反馈模型（仅考虑Pop III超新星作为X射线源）。

Result: Pop III超新星产生弱X射线背景和中等莱曼-维尔纳背景；X射线反馈总体上增加了Pop III数密度，在低密度区域增强更明显；实时计算比后处理更能体现反馈循环的重要性；X射线背景提高了高红移处的汤姆逊散射光学深度。

Conclusion: 即使是最保守的仅考虑Pop III超新星的X射线反馈模型，也能对Pop III恒星形成产生净正反馈效应，特别是在低密度区域。未来研究将纳入更多X射线源，预计会产生更强的反馈效应。

Abstract: In this paper, part of a series on the effects of X-ray sources in promoting Population III (Pop III) star formation, we investigate the ionisation and heating of the intergalactic medium (IGM) and the consequent enhancement of molecular hydrogen (H$_{2}$) and Pop III formation using cosmological zoom-in simulations. We adopt a minimal X-ray feedback model in which X-rays originate solely from Pop III supernovae, and compute the global X-ray and Lyman-Werner (LW) radiation backgrounds on-the-fly during the simulation of a mean-density region of the Universe. This approach self-consistently captures the feedback loop between Pop III stars and the radiation backgrounds they produce. Pop III supernovae generate a weak X-ray background (J$_{\mathrm{X,21}} \sim 10^{-5}$) and a moderate LW background (J$_{\mathrm{LW,21}} \sim 10^{-1}$); the latter intensifies below $z \approx 12$ (J$_{\mathrm{LW,21}} \sim 10^{1}-10^{2}$) with the onset of Pop II star formation. Applying these backgrounds to regions of varying mean density produces a net positive X-ray feedback that increases the Pop III number density, with stronger enhancement in underdense regions. The positive feedback is more pronounced when the X-ray background is computed on-the-fly rather than by post-processing, demonstrating the importance of the feedback loop. The X-ray background also raises the Thomson scattering optical depth at high redshift, while the total optical depth remains consistent with Planck 2018 constraints. Because our model includes only Pop III supernovae as X-ray sources, it represents the most conservative scenario; stronger X-ray feedback is expected when additional sources are included, as will be explored in future work.
