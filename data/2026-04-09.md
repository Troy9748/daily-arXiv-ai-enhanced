We moreover find that the collisional cooling with dark matter particles provides an efficient pathway for the formation of stellar binaries in the contracting cluster. The contraction is eventually slowed down due to the decreasing central dark matter densities and the formation of stellar binaries. Our models highlight that the dynamical processes governing the faintest galaxies give rise to a rich phenomenology, blurring the line between the dynamics of globular clusters and galaxies.
