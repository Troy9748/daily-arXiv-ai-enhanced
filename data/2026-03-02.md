### [9] [A Unified Origin of Primordial Black Hole Dark Matter and Nanohertz Gravitational Waves](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24061)
*Guillem Domènech,Shi Pi,Ao Wang*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 该研究提出一个统一框架，将Subaru-HSC观测到的超短时标微引力透镜事件与脉冲星计时阵列探测到的纳赫兹随机引力波背景联系起来，认为它们都源于原始曲率功率谱的平坦增强，支持原始黑洞构成全部暗物质的可能性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 最近Subaru-HSC的高频观测发现了超短时标微引力透镜事件，为行星质量原始黑洞构成全部暗物质提供了证据。同时，脉冲星计时阵列探测到了纳赫兹随机引力波背景。本研究旨在探索这两个看似不同的观测现象是否具有共同的原始起源。

Method: 提出一个统一的理论框架：假设原始曲率功率谱存在一个宽泛、近乎平坦的增强，振幅约为O(10^{-2})。这种增强会产生跨越行星质量到太阳质量范围的原始黑洞质量函数，同时产生可观测的引力波背景。

Result: 该框架能够自然地统一解释超短时标微引力透镜事件和纳赫兹随机引力波背景，产生的原始黑洞质量函数与所有当前观测约束一致。原始黑洞可以构成全部暗物质，同时产生观测到的引力波信号。

Conclusion: 原始黑洞-诱导引力波统一框架做出了具体的多信使预测，可以通过未来的微引力透镜巡天、下一代脉冲星计时阵列、空间干涉仪、精密天体测量和激光测距实验进行决定性检验。

Abstract: Recent high-cadence observations by Subaru-HSC have identified a population of ultrashort-timescale microlensing events, providing a compelling window for planet-mass primordial black holes (PBHs) to constitute the entirety of dark matter. In this Letter, we demonstrate that this PBH population and the nanohertz stochastic gravitational-wave (GW) background reported by pulsar timing arrays (PTAs) can be naturally unified by a single primordial origin: a broad, nearly-flat enhancement of the curvature power spectrum with an amplitude of $O(10^{-2})$. The resulting PBH mass function spans the planet-to-solar mass range, while remaining consistent with all current observational constraints. This unified PBH--induced-GW framework makes concrete multi-messenger predictions, which can be decisively scrutinized by forthcoming microlensing surveys, next-generation PTAs, space-borne interferometers, precision astrometry, and laser ranging experiments.

### [10] [Probing power spectrum enhancement at small scales with SKA](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24117)
*Hourui Zhu,Bin Yue,Yidong Xu,Xuelei Chen,Zhiqi Huang*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 该研究探讨了小尺度功率谱增强对宇宙再电离过程的影响，特别是对电离场形态和21厘米信号的效应。研究发现即使在高红移星系观测约束下，增强的小尺度功率谱仍能显著改变21厘米功率谱和电离泡尺寸分布。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机在于理解小尺度功率谱对宇宙再电离过程的影响。再电离主要由矮星系驱动，而迷你晕会消耗电离光子，这两者具有不同的聚类特性。小尺度功率谱的增强会同时影响电离源和汇，其净效应取决于二者竞争，因此21厘米信号可作为探测小尺度功率谱的有力工具。

Method: 采用Cielo等人（2025）提出的功率谱模型作为演示，研究小尺度功率谱增强对电离场和21厘米信号的影响。在观测到的高红移星系紫外光度函数和再电离历史的约束下，分析21厘米功率谱和电离泡尺寸分布的变化。

Result: 研究发现，对于C25模型，即使在观测约束下，21厘米功率谱和电离泡尺寸分布仍与标准ΛCDM模型存在显著差异。即将建成的SKA-low AA*望远镜及未来的成像望远镜有潜力更深入地探测小尺度功率谱。

Conclusion: 21厘米信号是探测小尺度功率谱的有效工具，小尺度功率谱增强会显著影响再电离过程的形态特征。未来的射电望远镜观测将能够更深入地研究这一现象，为理解早期宇宙结构形成提供新途径。

Abstract: The reionization process is driven by ionizing photons from dwarf galaxies in halos with virial temperature $T_{\rm vir} \gtrsim 10^4$ K, while minihalos whose $T_{\rm vir}\lesssim 10^4$ K consume ionizing photons and have negative contributions to reionization. Since ionizing sources and minihalos have different clustering characteristics, not only the reionization history, but also the morphology of the ionization field, is sensitive to the small-scale power spectrum. If the power spectrum at small scales is enhanced compared with the standard six-parameter $Λ$CDM model, then both the sources and sinks of ionizing photons would be boosted and the net impact depends on the competition between them. Therefore, the 21 cm signal that can probe the morphology of the ionization field will be a useful tool for detecting the small-scale power spectrum. Using the power spectrum proposed by Cielo et al. (2025) (C25) as a demonstration, we investigate the influence of small-scale power spectrum enhancement on the ionization field and the 21 cm signal. We find that for the C25 model, even under the constraints of observed UV luminosity functions for high-$z$ galaxies and reionization history, the 21~cm power spectrum and the bubble size distribution could be still significantly different from the regular $Λ$CDM model. The upcoming SKA-low AA* telescope, and a further imaging telescope, have the potential to detect the small-scale power spectrum more deeply.

### [11] [Weak lensing higher-order statistics to disentangle modified gravity and massive neutrinos](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24154)

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 弱引力透镜高阶统计量可区分广义相对论和修正引力理论，即使存在大质量中微子


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究弱引力透镜高阶统计量是否能够区分广义相对论和修正引力理论，特别是在存在大质量中微子的情况下，以突破宇宙学参数简并性限制

Method: 使用广义相对论和f(R)引力N体模拟生成的模拟收敛图，分析多种弱引力透镜高阶统计探针，采用不同度量标准评估各探针的区分能力，并改变测量设置

Result: 发现弱引力透镜高阶统计量确实能够区分修正引力和广义相对论，即使在存在大质量中微子的情况下也能有效工作

Conclusion: 弱引力透镜高阶统计量是区分不同引力理论的有力工具，能够突破传统二阶统计的限制，为宇宙学参数估计和引力理论测试提供新途径

Abstract: Going beyond second order in weak lensing (WL) statistics is known to break degeneracies among cosmological parameters. We take a step further here, investigating whether higher-order statistics (HOS) in weak lensing can disentangle among General Relativity (GR) and modified gravity (MG), also taking into account the presence of massive neutrinos. To this end, we rely on mock convergence maps obtained from GR and $f(R)$ gravity N - body simulations, and we look for MG signatures in a wide set of higher-order WL probes. We rely on different metrics to quantify the discriminatory power of each probe, also varying the measurement setup. We find out that WL HOS can indeed disentangle MG and GR also in the presence of massive neutrinos.

### [12] [Dark Acoustic Oscillations and the Hubble Tension](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23895)
*Mathias Garny,Florian Niedermann,Martin S. Sloth*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 通过暗辐射-物质解耦模型解决哈勃张力，预测了与重子声学振荡尺度相似的暗声学振荡信号，该预测与DESI异常数据一致


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 哈勃张力和DESI异常数据对标准ΛCDM宇宙学模型构成挑战，需要寻找替代方案

Method: 使用暗辐射-物质解耦模型解决哈勃张力，基于普朗克CMB测量和SH0ES校准的超新星数据，独立于大尺度结构数据进行推断

Result: 发现暗声学振荡信号，拖曳视界尺度为54-65 Mpc/h，振幅为0.02-0.05，与解释DESI异常所需参数一致

Conclusion: 暗辐射-物质解耦模型不仅为解决哈勃张力提供了方案，其预测的暗声学振荡信号也与DESI异常数据相符，为当前和未来的大尺度结构巡天提供了具体目标

Abstract: The Hubble tension and the recently reported anomaly in data from the Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument (DESI) are considered to pose serious challenges to the standard $Λ$CDM model of cosmology. In this work, we show that resolving the Hubble tension with a scenario featuring dark radiation-matter decoupling (DRMD) predicts the presence of dark acoustic oscillations (DAO) similar in scale to baryon acoustic oscillations (BAO). Using an inference independent of large-scale structure data, relying only on Planck measurements of the cosmic microwave background and SH$0$ES-calibrated supernova data, we find evidence for a DAO signal with drag-horizon scale $r_{d,\mathrm{DAO}} \in[54,65]\,\mathrm{Mpc}/h$ ($68\%\,\mathrm{C.I.}$) and amplitude $A_\mathrm{DAO} \in [0.02,0.05]$ ($68\%\,\mathrm{C.I.}$). These predictions provide a concrete target for current and upcoming large-scale structure surveys, including DESI, Euclid, and the Roman Space Telescope. Remarkably, the predicted DAO properties are consistent with those required to explain the DESI anomaly, offering both an alternative to evolving dark energy and a preliminary validation of the relevance of a dark radiation-matter decoupling scenario for addressing the Hubble tension.

### [13] [Discovery of a nearby radio relic in the low-mass, merging cluster Abell 4067](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24206)
*Isaac Magolego,Roger P. Deane,Kshitij Thorat,Ian Heywood,Justin Spilker,Taweewat Somboonpanyakul,Dazhi Zhou,Manuel Aravena,Joaquin D. Vieira,Kedar A. Phadke,Lindsey E. Bleem,Scott C. Chapman*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: MeerKAT望远镜在低质量星系团Abell 4067中发现了一个罕见的弧状射电遗迹，这是已知质量最低的星系团中发现的此类结构，为研究弱合并激波提供了新线索。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 星系团合并过程中产生的激波是研究宇宙大尺度结构形成和演化的重要探针。研究低质量星系团中的射电遗迹有助于理解不同质量星系团中合并事件的能量差异及其对射电辐射的影响。

Method: 使用MeerKAT望远镜的UHF波段（0.58-1.09 GHz）观测发现射电遗迹，结合XMM-Newton的X射线数据分析遗迹位置和方向，并与星系团内介质（ICM）的延伸方向进行对比验证。

Result: 在Abell 4067星系团（红移z=0.099）中发现了一个线性尺寸约1.48±0.02 Mpc的弧状射电遗迹，距离星系团中心0.95 Mpc。该遗迹在150 MHz的射电功率为3.10±0.03×10²⁴ W Hz⁻¹，比更高质量星系团中的遗迹更暗，表明与弱合并激波相关。未检测到中心射电晕。

Conclusion: 低质量星系团中的射电遗迹可能由能量较低的合并事件产生，形成弱合并激波。这一发现支持了明亮射电晕并非所有星系团合并的普遍产物，强调了星系团质量、合并能量和演化阶段在塑造星系团内介质中弥散射电辐射的重要作用。

Abstract: Shock waves generated during cluster mergers offer a powerful probe of how large-scale structure grows and evolves in the Universe. As part of the MeerKAT-South Pole Telescope (SPT) survey, we report the discovery of a single arc-like radio relic in the galaxy cluster Abell 4067 ($z=0.099$), one of the lowest-mass clusters known to host such a structure. MeerKAT UHF-band (0.58--1.09 GHz) observations reveal a relic with a largest linear size of $\sim 1.48 \pm 0.02$ Mpc, located at a projected distance of 0.95 Mpc from the cluster centre. XMM-Newton X-ray data show that the relic's position and orientation relative to the intracluster medium (ICM) elongation are consistent with a merger-driven shock-wave scenario. The relic has an estimated radio power of $3.10 \pm 0.03 \times 10^{24}$ W Hz$^{-1}$ at 150 MHz. When placed in the $P_{150\,\mathrm{MHz}}$--$M_{500}$ scaling relation, the Abell 4067 relic appears less luminous compared to relics in more massive clusters, suggesting an association with weak merger shocks. This finding supports the idea that relics in low-mass clusters may form through less energetic merger events, leading to weak merger shocks. This is further supported by the absence of a detectable central radio halo in Abell 4067, reinforcing the idea that luminous radio halos are not a universal outcome of cluster mergers and highlighting the role of cluster mass, merger energetics, and evolutionary stage in shaping diffuse radio emission in the intracluster medium.
