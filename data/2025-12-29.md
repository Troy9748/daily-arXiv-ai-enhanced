### [1] [Can A Kinematically Hot and Thick Disk Form A Bar? : Role of Highly Spinning Dark Matter Halos](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21632)
*Sandeep Kumar Kataria*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: JWST观测发现高红移宇宙中存在大量棒结构的热厚盘星系，这与当前对类似银河系星系盘稳定性的理解相矛盾。研究表明，在旋转暗晕环境中，经典稳定的热厚盘也能形成棒结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: JWST观测显示高红移宇宙中存在大量棒结构的热厚盘星系，这与传统理论认为热厚盘（色散主导）对棒形成稳定的观点相矛盾，需要重新理解高红移星系中棒形成的物理机制。

Method: 使用受控的N体模拟，研究位于非旋转和旋转暗晕中的热厚盘。比较两种暗晕环境下盘的稳定性，分析角动量传输效率，并测试现有棒形成判据的预测能力。

Result: 1. 在旋转暗晕环境中，经典稳定的热厚盘能够形成棒结构；2. 旋转暗晕模型从盘到晕的角动量传输效率比非旋转暗晕高8倍；3. Ostriker-Peebles和ELN判据无法预测两种暗晕模型的棒形成；4. Jang-Kim判据能成功预测非旋转暗晕模型的稳定性，但无法预测旋转暗晕模型的棒形成。

Conclusion: 旋转暗晕环境显著促进热厚盘中棒结构的形成，这为理解高红移宇宙中观测到的棒结构热厚盘星系提供了重要物理机制。现有棒形成判据在旋转暗晕环境下存在局限性，需要发展新的理论框架。

Abstract: Recent JWST observations claim the existence of a significant fraction of bars in the kinematically hotter and thicker disk at high redshift Universe. These observations challenge the current understanding of disk stability in galaxies similar to the Milky Way. The analytical work and N-body simulations suggest that the kinematically hot (dispersion-dominated) and thick disk are stable against bar formation. In this work, we perform the controlled N-body simulations of a kinematically hot and thick disk, which is residing in a non-rotating and spinning dark matter halo. We report that the disk, which is classically stable against bar instability in the live and non-rotating halo, leads to bar formation in a spinning halo environment. The spinning halo model is 8 times more efficient in transporting angular momentum from the disk to the halo compared to the non-spinning halo. We claim that Ostriker-Peebles and ELN bar formation criteria do not predict bar formation for both the non-roating and spinning halo. The recent criteria from Jang-Kim successfully predict the bar stability for the non-rotating halo model, but not for the spinning halo model. These results provide an important insight into the bar formation processes for thick and hot disks at high redshift.

Method: 结合Gaia和LAMOST数据获取具有完整三维速度信息的年轻O-B2星样本，聚焦银河系最大超气泡，分析其边缘星群的速度特征，计算各种时间尺度。

Result: 发现超气泡边缘O-B2星群具有25.8 km/s的横向速度和6.2 km/s的膨胀速度，计算得到穿越时间~20 Myr、膨胀时间尺度~80 Myr、生存时间尺度~250 Myr、超新星间隔~0.1 Myr，满足t_SN < t_shear < t_survival的关系。

Abstract: Stellar feedback is a crucial mechanism in galactic evolution, as demonstrated by the widespread bubbles observed with JWST. Together with the Galactic shear timescale t_shear ~ 30 Myr, these values satisfy t_SN < t_shear < t_survival. The energy and momentum from supernovae are sufficient to sustain the bubble's growth against ambient pressure. This indicates that repeated supernovae replenish energy faster than shear and turbulent distort the cavity. Our analysis classifies the Giant Oval Cavity as a large, quasi-stationary superbubble, similar to the Phantom Bubble observed by JWST, stabilised by the interplay between stellar feedback and Galactic disk dynamics.

### [5] [The rotation curve of the Milky Way measured by Classical Cepheids from Gaia DR3](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21780)
*Qikang Feng,Yang Huang,Huawei Zhang,Jifeng Liu*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 利用903颗经典造父变星构建银河系旋转曲线，发现10-11kpc处有凹陷、13-14kpc处有凸起特征，结合先前数据得到太阳位置圆周速度236.8km/s，并估算暗物质密度和晕质量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 精确测量银河系旋转曲线以更好地理解银河系质量分布和暗物质特性，特别是利用造父变星作为年轻示踪物来探测旋转曲线的精细结构。

Method: 使用Gaia DR3中903颗经典造父变星的精确自行运动和径向速度数据，通过周期-韦森海特关系确定距离，从三维速度分量计算旋转曲线，并与其他测量结果结合构建平均旋转曲线。

Result: 旋转曲线在6-18kpc范围内总体呈轻微下降趋势，但在10-11kpc处出现凹陷，13-14kpc处出现凸起；太阳位置圆周速度为236.8±0.8 km/s；局部暗物质密度为0.33-0.40 GeV/cm³，18kpc内暗物质晕质量为1.19-1.45×10¹¹ M☉。

Conclusion: 造父变星是研究银河系旋转曲线的有效示踪物，旋转曲线的凹陷-凸起特征可能是银河系结构的真实特征；通过平均旋转曲线得到的质量模型与先前测量一致，为银河系质量分布提供了新约束。

Abstract: We determine the rotation curve (RC) of the Milky Way in the range 6 < R < 18 kpc using a sample of 903 carefully selected classical Cepheids with precise proper motions and high-quality radial velocities from \emph{Gaia} DR3. Their distances can be accurately measured from the well-known Period-Wesenheit relations. The RC is computed from the three-dimensional velocity components of these Cepheids. Generally, the RC shows a slight decline with distance from the Galactic center. On top of this general trend, the newly constructed RC shows a dip around R ~ 10-11 kpc, followed by a bump around R ~ 13-14 kpc. This feature has also been reported in other RC measurements, mostly in RCs traced by young tracers like Cepheids. To better constrain the Milky Way mass, an averaged RC is then constructed by combining measurements from this work and previous efforts. Due to the ambiguous nature of the dip-and-bump feature, this averaged RC is constructed only within the radial range where the RC appears to be less influenced by this feature. By using this averaged RC, we determine the circular velocity at solar position and also build a parameterized mass model of our Milky Way. The result for the circular velocity at the solar position is $V_c(R_0) = 236.8 \pm 0.8\ \mathrm{km\,s^{-1}}$, which is in good agreement with previous measurements. The local dark matter density and the enclosed dark matter halo mass within 18 kpc are estimated from the averaged RC under different baryonic models, yielding a series of consistent results: a local density of $0.33-0.40\ \mathrm{GeV\,cm^{-3}}$ and an enclosed mass of $1.19-1.45 \times 10^{11}\ M_\odot$.

### [6] [Implications of Cosmic Birefringence for Multi-Field ALP Dark Matter](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21888)
*Jialiang Shao,Ippei Obata,Dongdong Zhang*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 双ALP场模型可以缓解宇宙双折射观测对超轻轴子类粒子的限制，解决单场模型中的washout效应问题。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 宇宙双折射是检验标准宇宙学模型之外理论的重要探针，轴子类粒子是重要的暗物质候选者。先前单场ALP模型因washout效应（标量场振荡动力学导致的CMB偏振功率谱抑制）而被排除，需要更一般的理论框架来克服这一限制。

Method: 开发了一个包含两个ALP场的更一般理论框架，提供了分析近似和数值计算方法，研究两个不同质量的ALP场的叠加效应。

Axion-like particles (ALPs), as promising dark matter candidates, possess unique advantages in naturally explaining such phenomena. Previous studies on the implications of cosmic birefringence for these ultralight ALP fields have focused on single-field models with conventional potentials. To address this limitation, we develop a more general theoretical framework incorporating two ALP fields, providing analytical approximations and numerical calculations. Our findings reveal that the superposition of two ALP fields with distinct masses can relax the constraints imposed by the washout effect and reconcile with observations.
