### [1] [Beyond $Λ$CDM: fundamental constants as cosmological observables](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10987)
*Dinko Milaković,John K. Webb*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 该论文提出通过测量精细结构常数α的变化来检验ΛCDM模型之外的物理，利用WST等设施可获得大量α测量数据，为区分不同宇宙学模型提供关键约束。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前宇宙学中的张力对ΛCDM模型构成挑战，但即将到来的观测设施主要探测引力相互作用，对非引力相互作用信息有限。需要多样化的物理测试来区分预测相似但基本原理不同的竞争模型。

Method: 通过测量精细结构常数α的时空变化来检验超出ΛCDM的物理。使用两种方法：1）利用多线法测量类星体吸收系统；2）测量星系中发射线气体的原子双线。WST等光谱设施可产生超过10万个类星体α测量和100万个星系α测量。

Result: 论文未提供具体实验结果，但预测WST等设施将能产生大规模、均匀的α测量数据集，结合其他探测手段，将为超出ΛCDM的物理提供前所未有的约束。

Conclusion: 观测基本常数时空变化是区分不同宇宙学模型的关键测试。大规模α测量数据集结合其他探测手段，将能有效约束超出ΛCDM的物理，为解决当前宇宙学张力提供重要线索。

Abstract: Recent cosmological tensions pose difficulties for $Λ$CDM. Forthcoming facilities will be able to check whether these tensions result from systematic effects or indeed with the $Λ$CDM model itself. However, these new data will primarily probe gravitational interactions and provide only limited information about non-gravitational interactions. Distinguishing between competing models that make similar predictions yet rely on fundamentally different principles, therefore requires suitably diverse physical tests. Observational constraints on spacetime variations of fundamental constants fill this need. The fine-structure constant, $α= e^2/\hbar c$, can be measured using absorption systems towards bright quasars using the Many Multiplet method, and using atomic doublets from line emitting gas in galaxies. A spectroscopic facility such as the WST could produce more than 100,000 new measurements of $α$ from quasars together with a million measurements from galaxies. When combined with other probes, such a large and homogeneous dataset of $α$ measurements would provide unprecedented constraints on physics beyond $Λ$CDM.

### [2] [Massive stars as gravitationally lensed transients -- Insights on the high-mass initial mass function](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10909)
*Sung Kei Li*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: 通过引力透镜星系中瞬态透镜星的观测探测率来研究恒星初始质量函数，目前未发现z≈1星系存在顶部重IMF的证据。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 恒星初始质量函数（IMF）对恒星形成研究至关重要，但直接测量仅限于本地宇宙。需要开发新方法来研究遥远星系的IMF，以突破望远镜分辨率的限制。

Method: 利用强引力透镜星系中瞬态透镜星的观测探测率作为探针。这些是大质量、高亮度的恒星，通过微引力透镜暂时可被探测。该方法可以打破光谱能量分布拟合中IMF与恒星形成历史的简并性。

Result: 目前研究显示，在红移z≈1的星系中，没有发现顶部重IMF（即大质量恒星比例偏高的IMF）的证据。

Conclusion: 瞬态透镜星探测率为研究遥远星系的恒星初始质量函数提供了新窗口，初步结果表明z≈1星系的IMF与本地宇宙相似，未表现出顶部重特征。

Abstract: A robust stellar initial mass function (IMF) is crucial in any studies related to star formation. However, the direct measurement of the stellar IMF is confined to the local universe, limited by the resolving power of telescopes. Recently, a new method for accessing the stellar IMF beyond the local universe has been developed. The observed detection rate of transient lensed stars -- individual, massive, thus luminous stars in strongly lensed galaxies that are temporarily detectable upon stellar microlensing -- can serve as a probe to break the IMF-star formation history degeneracy in studies utilizing spectral energy distribution fitting, hence providing a window to look at the IMF at a subsample of gravitationally lensed galaxies. In this proceeding, I summarize the contributed talk given at IAUS402 entitled the same as this contribution and highlight some key results, which currently show no evidence for a top-heavy IMF in $z \approx 1$ galaxies.

### [3] [The Keck/DEIMOS Stellar Archive: II. Dynamical Masses and Metallicities for a Uniform Sample of Milky Way Satellites](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10202)
*Marla Geha*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 研究人员对67个银河系卫星星系和球状星团进行了均匀的光谱分析，获得了动力学质量、金属丰度等关键参数，揭示了卫星星系与球状星团在质量-光比上的明显差异，并发现了恒星质量-金属丰度关系在log M*/Msun = 4附近的转折点。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 以往研究银河系卫星星系时，不同光谱分析流程和成员星判定标准存在系统性差异，这些差异可能被误认为天体物理信号或掩盖真实趋势。为了获得更可靠的天体物理约束（如暗物质、星系形成阈值），需要建立自洽、均匀的光谱分析样本。

Method: 对Keck/DEIMOS光谱仪观测的单个恒星进行均匀再分析，确定了67个系统（每个系统至少有10颗成员星）的动力学质量、平均[Fe/H]金属丰度及金属丰度弥散度。通过比较卫星星系和球状星团的质量-光比等参数进行分类研究。

Result: 1. 在相同恒星质量下，卫星星系与球状星团在动力学质量和质量-光比上明显分离；2. 卫星星系的平均封闭质量密度与半解析CDM模型预测一致；3. 在log M*/Msun = 4附近发现恒星质量-金属丰度关系的转折点：高于此质量时金属丰度随质量减小而降低（弥散度0.16 dex），低于此质量时关系变平且/或弥散度增加；4. 卫星星系内部金属丰度弥散度在0.3-0.4 dex范围内。

Conclusion: 这项均匀测量工作将为恒星质量-晕质量关系提供更严格的约束，改进暗物质搜索的J因子估计，并为LSST/Roman/Euclid时代预期发现的大量新银河系卫星星系的解释奠定基础。

Abstract: Population-level studies of Milky Way satellites used to constrain dark matter or the threshold of galaxy formation often rely on velocity dispersions and metallicities derived from heterogeneous spectroscopic analyses. Systematic differences between data reduction pipelines and membership criteria can masquerade as astrophysical signals, or obscure real trends. Here, we present the largest self-consistent sample of spectroscopically-derived quantities for Milky Way satellite galaxies and globular clusters based on a homogeneous re-analysis of individual stars observed with the Keck/DEIMOS spectrograph. We determine enclosed dynamical masses, mean [Fe/H] metallicities, and metallicity dispersions for 67 systems with 10 or more member stars. At a given stellar mass, systems classified as satellite galaxies are well separated from globular clusters in their dynamical mass and mass-to-light ratios. The average enclosed mass densities of satellite galaxies agree with semi-analytic CDM model predictions. For satellite galaxies, we observe a break in the stellar mass-metallicity relation near log M*/Msun = 4 (M_V ~ -4.5). Above this stellar mass, satellite galaxies show the well-known tight trend (0.16 dex scatter in [Fe/H]) of decreasing metallicity with stellar mass; below log M*/Msun = 4, the mass-metallicity relation flattens and/or increases in scatter. Satellite galaxies have internal metallicity scatter between 0.3-0.4 dex across our stellar mass range. These uniform measurements will enable tighter constraints on the stellar mass-halo mass relation, improved J-factor estimates for dark matter searches, and lay a foundation for interpreting the flood of new Milky Way satellites expected in the LSST/Roman/Euclid era.

### [4] [The stellar-to-halo mass relation of central galaxies across three orders of halo mass](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10193)
*Victoria Toptun,Paola Popesso,Ilaria Marini,Stephan Vladutescu-Zopp,Klaus Dolag,Peter Behroozi,Lorenzo Lovisari,Stefano Ettori,Veronica Biffi,Xiaohu Yang,Natanael de Isídio,Daudi T. Mazengo*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该研究利用eROSITA X射线数据和SDSS光学数据，首次通过X射线方法测量了从星系到星系团尺度（10^12-10^15 M⊙）的中央最亮星系（BCG）恒星质量与暗晕质量关系（SHMR），发现关系在M_halo~10^12 M⊙处达到峰值，随后下降，反映了反馈过程对恒星形成效率的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 星系恒星含量与其宿主暗晕质量和增长密切相关，但目前对低质量星系团的观测约束有限，导致对星系在不同暗晕质量尺度上如何组装恒星存在不确定性。准确测量BCG恒星质量-暗晕质量关系对于理解星系形成和反馈过程的作用至关重要。

Method: 使用eROSITA eRASS1 X射线数据获取暗晕质量，结合SDSS测光数据测量BCG恒星质量。通过叠加光学选星系团的X射线光谱，获得每个质量区间的平均晕气体温度，然后通过M-T_X关系转换为暗晕质量，从而构建跨越三个数量级暗晕质量范围的SHMR。

Result: 发现SHMR在M_halo~10^12 M⊙处达到峰值，随后在高质量端恒星比例下降。这一趋势反映了AGN反馈、冷却效率降低以及外源组装主导等因素共同作用，降低了高质量暗晕中的恒星形成效率。结果与弱引力透镜、X射线分析和动力学方法等先前观测研究一致，但与流体动力学模拟在星系团尺度上存在显著差异。

Conclusion: 该研究首次提供了基于X射线的观测性SHMR，覆盖三个数量级的暗晕质量范围，为测试星系形成模型建立了稳健的基准。结果表明BCG恒星增长对反馈机制和暗晕组装历史高度敏感，揭示了高质量暗晕中恒星形成效率降低的物理机制。

Abstract: The stellar content of galaxies is tightly connected to the mass and growth of their host dark matter halos. Observational constraints on this relation remain limited, particularly for low-mass groups, leaving uncertainties in how galaxies assemble their stars across halo mass scales. Accurately measuring the brightest central galaxy (BCG) stellar-to-halo mass relation (SHMR) over a wide mass range is therefore crucial for understanding galaxy formation and the role of feedback processes. Here we present the SHMR spanning $M_{\rm halo} \sim 10^{12}$-$10^{15}\,M_\odot$, using halo masses derived from eROSITA eRASS1 X-ray data and BCG stellar masses based on SDSS photometry. By stacking X-ray spectra of optically selected groups, we recover robust average halo gas temperatures for each bin, which are then converted to halo masses via the $M$-$T_X$ relation. We find that the SHMR peaks near $M_{\rm halo} \sim 10^{12}\,M_\odot$, with a declining stellar fraction at higher masses. This trend reflects a combination of processes that reduce the efficiency of stellar mass growth in massive halos, such as AGN feedback, reduced cooling efficiency, and the increasing dominance of ex-situ assembly, while halos continue to grow through mergers and accretion. Our measurements are consistent over the full mass range with previous observational studies, including weak lensing, X-ray analyses of individual clusters, and kinematical and dynamical methods. Comparisons with hydrodynamical simulations show good agreement at low masses but reveal significant discrepancies in the normalization at cluster scales, highlighting the sensitivity of BCG stellar growth to feedback prescriptions and halo assembly history. These results provide the first X-ray-based observational SHMR covering three orders of magnitude in halo mass, establish a robust benchmark for testing galaxy formation models.

</details>
