### [1] [Stochastic Evolution of Galactic Star Formation with Halo Coupling, AGN Quenching and Hopf Bifurcation Dynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23077)
*Sanjeev Kumar,A. K. Awasthi,Mahesh Kumar*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 提出基于随机非线性振荡器的星系演化计算框架，通过超临界Hopf分岔捕捉从静止稳定到合并驱动星暴的转变，噪声诱导的星暴在确定性阈值以下发生，揭示星系心跳周期性特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立最小化但可解释的框架，将局部反馈和全局势能与宏观星系演化联系起来，解释星系从静止到星暴的转变过程。

Method: 使用耦合随机非线性振荡器（随机Hopf引擎），通过Euler-Maruyama方法模拟暗物质晕特性的乘性噪声，采用Fokker-Planck方程分析星暴振幅统计特性。

Result: 模拟揭示星系心跳周期性特征，噪声抑制平均恒星形成率但增强间歇性，包含剪切和空间变化分岔场可重现螺旋形态和AGN驱动的淬灭。

Conclusion: 该框架提供了连接局部反馈、全局势能和宏观星系演化的最小化可解释模型，暗物质晕散射通过增强间歇性影响星系演化统计特性。

Abstract: We present a computational framework for galactic evolution based on a coupled stochastic nonlinear oscillator, implemented with the \textbf{Stochastic Hopf Engine}. Gas density ($G$) and star formation rate ($S$) co-evolve through a supercritical Hopf bifurcation, capturing the transition from quiescent stability to merger-driven starbursts. Scatter in dark matter halo properties, modeled as multiplicative noise via the \textbf{Euler--Maruyama method}, broadens the bifurcation into a regime where noise-induced bursts occur below the deterministic threshold.
  Simulations reveal a periodic signature, the \textbf{Galactic Heartbeat}, emerging as a deterministic limit cycle validated by the \textbf{data3} resonance peak in the star-formation spectrum. A radial reduction yields an effective \textbf{Fokker--Planck equation} for burst amplitude; its stationary solution matches numerical PDFs, providing statistical closure.
  Including differential shear $Ω(r)$ and spatially varying bifurcation fields reproduces spiral morphologies and AGN-driven quenching. Driving the growth parameter sub-critical ($r_{agn} < 0$) yields ``Red and Dead'' cores via attractor collapse. Dark matter halo scatter suppresses mean star formation while enhancing intermittency, offering a minimal yet interpretable framework linking local feedback and global potentials to macroscopic galactic evolution.

### [2] [Distribution functions for spheroids](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23127)
*James Binney*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 论文研究了基于作用量积分的分布函数构建自洽星系模型的方法，指出了热成分分布函数在J_φ趋近于0时需要满足特定条件以避免非物理速度分布，并探讨了径向偏向球形系统对四极扰动的固有不稳定性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机在于构建包含暗物质等多组分的自洽引力束缚星系模型，这些模型基于作用量积分的解析分布函数。需要解决现有模型中可能存在的非物理速度分布问题，并探索不同各向异性结构模型的构建方法。

Method: 采用基于作用量积分J的解析分布函数方法构建星系模型。研究了各向同性和径向偏向球形系统的分布函数构造，以及扁平化系统中显著速度各向异性的分布函数构建。通过自洽模型而非外部势场约束的种群构建来分析系统稳定性。

Result: 研究发现热成分分布函数在J_φ趋近于0时需要满足特定条件以避免非物理速度分布。成功构建了各向同性和径向偏向球形系统的分布函数，以及具有显著速度各向异性的扁平系统分布函数。发现径向偏向球形系统对四极扰动具有固有不稳定性。

Conclusion: 基于作用量积分的分布函数是构建自洽星系模型的有效方法，但需要特别注意热成分在J_φ趋近于0时的条件。径向偏向球形系统存在固有稳定性问题，而混沌可能在盘增长过程中维持这些约束条件。

Abstract: Galaxy models comprising several components (including dark matter) that are bound by the self-consistently generated gravitational field are readily constructed from distribution functions (DFs) that are analytic functions of the action integrals J. We explain why such models have unphysical velocity distributions unless the DFs of hot components satisfy certain conditions as J_φ-> 0. We show how DFs for both isotropic and radially biased spherical systems can be constructed with specified f(J). We show how to construct DFs for flattened systems with significant velocity anisotropy. Construction of self-consistent models rather than populations that are confined by an external potential leads to the conclusion that radially-biased spherical systems are generically unstable to quadrupolar perturbations. Chaos is likely key to maintenance of these constraints during adiabatic disc growth.

### [3] [Cosmic Environment as the Primary Driver of Dwarf Satellite Statistics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22485)
*Saeed Tavasoli,Parsa Ghafour*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该研究通过模拟分析卫星矮星系分布，发现其丰度主要受环境调控，宿主星系质量与宇宙时期为次要因素，为未来巡天观测提供预测基准。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 卫星矮星系的丰度和空间分布能反映宿主星系性质和大尺度环境，对理解星系形成演化至关重要。本研究旨在量化卫星星系种群对宿主恒星质量、形态和恒星形成活动的依赖性，并在ΛCDM框架下追踪其宇宙时间演化。

Method: 使用Millennium模拟结合半解析模型，构建宿主星系（Mr < -16）及其卫星星系（M_* ≥ 3×10^5 M_sun，Mr < -9）的一致样本。分析团、群和空洞环境中卫星星系的丰度和径向分布，追踪从z=2到z=0在三个宿主恒星质量区间内的演化。

Result: 卫星丰度与宿主恒星质量和核球质量强相关，控制恒星质量后宿主形态影响很小。致密环境相对空洞抑制卫星种群。z=0时径向分布显示：空洞中强中心聚集，团中扁平分布，群中呈中间趋势。红移演化显示：致密环境中低中质量宿主逐渐扁平化，大质量宿主保持稳定，空洞中中心聚集增强。卫星丰度的宇宙演化呈现不同路径：空洞中逐渐积累，群中质量依赖趋势，团中晚期强烈抑制。

Conclusion: 卫星星系的分布和丰度主要受环境调控，宿主恒星质量和宇宙时期为次要调节因素。LSST、Euclid和Roman Space Telescope等未来宽视场巡天将为这些预测提供严格检验。

Abstract: Context:Satellite dwarf galaxies provide key constraints on galaxy formation and evolution as their abundance and spatial distribution reflect both host properties and large-scale environment.
  Aims:This study quantifies the dependence of satellite populations on host stellar mass, morphology and star formation activity across diverse environments and traces their evolution with cosmic time within the LCDM framework.
  Methods:The Millennium simulation combined with the semi-analytic model is employed to construct consistent samples of host galaxies brighter than Mr < -16 and their satellites (M_* >= 3x10^5 M_sun, Mr < -9) within their virial radius. Satellite abundance and radial profiles are analyzed in cluster, group and void environments and their evolution is traced from z=2 to z=0 across three host stellar mass bins.
  Results:Satellite abundance correlates strongly with host stellar and bulge mass while host morphology has little additional impact once stellar mass is controlled for. Dense environments suppress satellite populations relative to voids. At z=0 radial profiles reveal strong central concentrations in voids flattened distributions in clusters and intermediate trends in groups. Their redshift evolution shows progressive flattening for low- and intermediate-mass hosts in dense environments stability for massive hosts and increasing central concentration in voids. The cosmic evolution of satellite abundance further highlights distinct pathways: gradual accumulation in voids, mass-dependent trends in groups and strong late-time suppression in clusters.
  Conclusions:The distribution and abundance of satellite galaxies are governed primarily by environment with host stellar mass and cosmic epoch acting as secondary modulators. Forthcoming wide-field surveys such as LSST, Euclid and the Roman Space Telescope are expected to provide stringent tests of these predictions.
