### [1] [A novel sub-grid model for super-Eddington accretion of spinning black holes in galaxy-scale simulations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.19281)
*Wei-Bo Kao,Pedro R. Capelo,Elia Cenci,Lucio Mayer,Alessandro Lupi,Luca Sala*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该研究提出了一个用于超爱丁顿吸积的黑洞质量和自旋演化子网格模型，通过模拟发现角动量错位是维持超爱丁顿吸积的关键条件，这有助于解释早期宇宙中超大质量黑洞的形成。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 解释早期宇宙中质量超过十亿太阳质量的黑洞如何在大爆炸后十亿年内形成，超爱丁顿吸积被认为是可能的机制，但需要更精确的模型来研究其可持续性条件。

Method: 开发了一个基于吸积盘的子网格模型，包含内层光子捕获区和外层薄α盘，整合了自旋演化机制（Bardeen-Petterson效应和厚盘进动），并在GIZMO流体动力学代码中实现。通过模拟黑洞嵌入气体环核盘和球形恒星分布的环境，研究超爱丁顿吸积的可持续条件。

Result: 模拟显示，当气体流入与初始对齐的黑洞-盘系统存在角动量错位时，能产生极高的爱丁顿比率，这是因为流入能快速移除盘的角动量。这表明超爱丁顿吸积需要高度动态的环境，这在红移较高的星系中更可能发生。

Conclusion: 角动量错位是实现超爱丁顿吸积的关键因素，该模型为中等质量黑洞种子生长到足以解释高红移明亮类星体的大小提供了可能途径，强调了高度动态环境对触发超爱丁顿吸积事件的重要性。

Abstract: Super-Eddington accretion has been proposed to explain the existence of black holes (BHs) with masses exceeding a billion solar masses within the first billion years after the Big Bang. We present a novel accretion disc-based sub-grid model for BH mass and spin evolution in the super-Eddington regime, implemented in the hydrodynamics code GIZMO. In our model, motivated by results of radiation-hydrodynamics simulations of accretion discs, the growth of the BH is mediated by a sub-grid accretion disc, comprising an inner photon-trapping region described by simulation-based fitting formulae and an outer thin $α$-disc with three regions. We incorporate a self-consistent spin evolution prescription that transitions between the Bardeen-Petterson effect and inner thick-disc precession, depending on the accretion rate. We perform a suite of idealised simulations of a BH embedded in a gaseous circumnuclear disc and a spherically distributed stellar component to explore the conditions under which super-Eddington accretion can be sustained in the environment of a realistic galactic nucleus. Simulations with misaligned gas inflows onto an initially aligned BH-disc system yield very high Eddington ratios, triggered by the rapid removal of disc angular momentum via inflows. These results highlight the importance of angular momentum misalignment in enabling super-Eddington accretion and suggest that such episodes are difficult to trigger unless the system resides in a highly dynamical environment -- a condition more likely to occur in high-redshift galaxies. Our model potentially provides a way to grow moderate-mass BH seeds to the sizes required to explain the bright high-redshift quasars.

### [2] [BASS.L. Near-infrared Data Release 3: A Spectral Atlas and Characterization of AGN](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.20702)
*Jarred Gillette,Michael J. Koss,Darshan Kakkad,Federica Ricci,Isabella Lamperti,Kyuseok Oh,Alejandra Rojas,Yaherlyn Diaz,Turgay Caglar,Kohei Ichikawa,Ignacio del Moral-Castro,Claudio Ricci,Ezequiel Treister,Franz E. Bauer,Richard Mushotzky,Mislav Baloković,Jakob S. den Brok,Benny Trakhtenbrot,C. Megan Urry,Fiona Harrison,Daniel Stern*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该研究分析了453个硬X射线选AGN的近红外光谱数据，发现近红外日冕线比光学[OIII]能更可靠地示踪AGN光度，并提出了改进的近红外诊断图来区分AGN、恒星形成和复合源。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究硬X射线选AGN的近红外发射线性质、AGN诊断和核周气体动力学，以更好地理解AGN活动、被遮蔽的宽线区以及AGN与周围环境的相互作用。

Method: 使用BAT AGN光谱巡天近红外数据发布3中的453个硬X射线选AGN样本，通过VLT X-shooter多波长光谱仪（0.3-2.5μm）进行观测，采用≥2σ检测阈值，分析近红外发射线特征。

Result: 近红外日冕线（特别是SiVI λ1.964）与硬X射线光度相关性更强（σ=0.25dex），比光学[OIII] λ5007（σ=0.55dex）更可靠；宽帕邢线在12%的Seyfert 2和57%的Seyfert 1.9中被探测到；提出了改进的近红外诊断图；发现热分子气体质量与X射线光度存在中等相关性。

Conclusion: 近红外光谱在探测AGN活动、被遮蔽的宽线区以及AGN与核周环境的复杂相互作用方面具有重要价值，为理解AGN反馈和分子气体激发过程提供了新见解。

Abstract: We present an analysis of near-infrared (NIR) emission-line properties, AGN diagnostics, and circumnuclear gas dynamics for 453 hard X-ray selected (14$-$195 keV) AGN from the BAT AGN Spectroscopic Survey (BASS) NIR Data Release 3 (DR3; $\langle z \rangle=0.036$, $z<1.0$). This dataset is the largest compilation of rest-frame NIR spectroscopic observations of hard-X-ray-selected AGN and includes the full DR2 sample. Observations were obtained with VLT X-shooter, a multiwavelength (0.3$-$2.5$μ$m) spectrograph ($R$ = 4,000$-$18,000), using a $\geq 2 σ$ detection threshold, enabling broad analysis of emission features. We find that NIR coronal lines, particularly SiVI $\lambda1.964$, are more reliable tracers of AGN luminosity than optical [OIII], showing a tighter correlation with hard X-ray luminosity ($σ=0.25$dex) than [OIII] $\lambda5007$ ($σ=0.55$dex). Broad Paschen lines (Pa$α$ and Pa$β$) are detected in 12% of Seyfert 2 and 57% of Seyfert 1.9, consistent with previous hidden BLR studies. We introduce a refined NIR diagnostic diagram (FeII $\lambda1.257$$μ$m/Pa$β$ and H$_2$ $λ2.122$$μ$m/Br$γ$) that effectively distinguishes AGN, star-forming, and composite sources even when contamination limits individual diagnostics or only upper limits are available. Additionally, we find a moderate correlation ($p \approx 7.4 \times 10^{-3}$) between hot molecular gas mass (traced by H$_2$ 2.121$μ$m) and X-ray luminosity, while its relation with Eddington ratio is weaker. The hot-to-cold gas mass ratio spans four orders of magnitude, averaging $\sim 3 \times 10^{-7}$, indicating diverse molecular gas excitation processes likely driven by star formation and AGN feedback. Our results underscore the value of NIR spectroscopy in probing AGN activity, obscured BLRs, and the complex interactions between AGN and their circumnuclear environments.
