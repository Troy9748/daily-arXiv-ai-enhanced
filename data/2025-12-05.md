Motivation: 弱引力透镜宇宙学一直受到重子反馈和本征排列等天体物理不确定性的限制，这些系统误差阻碍了从弱透镜数据中提取非线性信息的能力。

Method: 使用外部数据校准这些效应：1）通过直接光谱测量校准"蓝色"恒星形成星系样本的本征排列模型参数；2）利用X射线气体分数和运动学Sunyaev-Zel'dovich效应剖面观测来校准重子反馈模型，覆盖广泛的晕质量和红移范围。

Result: 获得S8=0.832^{+0.013}_{-0.017}的2%精度约束，比DES Y3基准模型的约束精度提高2倍，结果向CMB测量值偏移1.5σ，更符合标准六参数ΛCDM宇宙学。

Conclusion: 该方法为下一代巡天提供了蓝图：利用星系性质控制本征排列，借助外部气体探针校准反馈效应，从而显著提升弱透镜巡天的精度。

Abstract: Cosmology from weak gravitational lensing has been limited by astrophysical uncertainties in baryonic feedback and intrinsic alignments. By calibrating these effects using external data, we recover non-linear information, achieving a 2% constraint on the clustering amplitude, $S_8$, resulting in a factor of two improvement on the $Λ$CDM constraints relative to the fiducial Dark Energy Survey Year 3 model. The posterior, $S_8=0.832^{+0.013}_{-0.017}$, shifts by $1.5σ$ to higher values, in closer agreement with the cosmic microwave background result for the standard six-parameter $Λ$CDM cosmology. Our approach uses a star-forming 'blue' galaxy sample with intrinsic alignment model parameters calibrated by direct spectroscopic measurements, together with a baryonic feedback model informed by observations of X-ray gas fractions and kinematic Sunyaev-Zel'dovich effect profiles that span a wide range in halo mass and redshift. Our results provide a blueprint for next-generation surveys: leveraging galaxy properties to control intrinsic alignments and external gas probes to calibrate feedback, unlocking a substantial improvement in the precision of weak lensing surveys.
