Result: 在气相中识别出低温星际条件下CH₃CHS的无势垒形成机制。表面环境以位点特异性方式调节活化能。与氧类似物相比，硫化学因S-H键较弱而提供替代路径，在冰粒表面形成竞争性路径生成乙烷-1,1-二硫醇(CH₃CH(SH)SH)，可能降低CH₃CHS产率。首次报告了硫代乙醇在水冰上的准确结合能，确认其挥发性高于乙醇。

Conclusion: 提出的机制为TMC-1、猎户座和Sgr B2(N)中CH₃CH₂SH和CH₃CHS明显相互排斥的检测提供了初步假设，需要进一步通过定量天体化学建模验证，并与观测灵敏度限制区分。这些定性发现突出了含硫有机物在星际介质中的多方面化学行为，支持CH₃CH(SH)SH作为有前景的天体化学目标。

To mimic interstellar ice-mantles, we use medium (W6) and large amorphized (W22) water clusters as implemented in Binding Energy Evaluation protocol. A barrierless formation mechanism for CH$_{3}$CHS under low-temperature interstellar conditions is identified, in the gas phase. Surface environments modulate activation barriers in a site-specific manner, elucidated through both Langmuir-Hinshelwood and Eley-Rideal initiated surface reaction pathways. The proposed mechanism offers a tentative hypothesis for the apparent mutual exclusive detections of the CH$_{3}$CH$_{2}$SH and CH$_{3}$CHS in TMC-1, Orion, and Sgr B2(N), that further requires validation through quantitative astrochemical modeling and also to distinguish this chemical differentiation from observational sensitivity limitations. These qualitative findings highlight the multifaceted chemical behavior of sulfur-bearing organics in the interstellar medium and support CH$_{3}$CH(SH)SH as promising astro-chemical targets.
